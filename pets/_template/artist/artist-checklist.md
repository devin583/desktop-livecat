# Artist Checklist

- [ ] Character is original and does not copy protected mascots.
- [ ] Full body is visible.
- [ ] Preview image exists.
- [ ] PSD is RGB, 8 bit/channel, sRGB.
- [ ] Background is transparent.
- [ ] Left and right eyes are separate.
- [ ] Eye whites, irises, highlights, and eyelids are separate.
- [ ] Left and right ears are separate.
- [ ] Front paws are separate.
- [ ] Back paws are separate.
- [ ] Tail is separated into base, middle, and tip.
- [ ] Prop layers are separate from character layers.
- [ ] Hidden overlap art is painted under moving parts.
- [ ] Layer names are stable.
- [ ] Notes for expressions and motions are written in `asset-brief.md`.
