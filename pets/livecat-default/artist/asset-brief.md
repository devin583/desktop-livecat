# Mochi Prototype Asset Brief

## Character

- Original round-faced peach and cream desktop cat.
- Compact body, short legs, curled segmented tail.
- Sits beside a dark plum mini keyboard.
- Personality: focused, soft, slightly sleepy, helpful.

## Palette

- Fur: warm peach, cream, soft rose.
- Eyes and line accents: dark plum.
- Keyboard: dark plum body, warm cream keys.
- Timer note: warm paper yellow.

## Expressions

- Idle: relaxed open eyes, tiny smile.
- Focus: lowered eyelids, eyes aimed toward keyboard/user.
- Break: happy stretch, blush visible.
- Sleepy: half-closed eyes, relaxed mouth.

## Motion Notes

- Front paws must land on keyboard home keys.
- Ears should twitch independently.
- Tail needs three separate sections for secondary motion.
- Keyboard body can be mostly rigid, but selected keys must press downward.
